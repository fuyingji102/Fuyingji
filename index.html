Biography

I am currently a third year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng. Previously, I received my MS from The Johns Hopkins University in 2013, and BS from Zhejiang University in 2011, both with a specialization in biomedical engineering.

My research interests include medical image computing and machine learning.
